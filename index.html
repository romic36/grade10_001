Word Processing Fundamentals & an Introduction to AI

Total Time Remaining

40:00
Intro (5m) Presentation (15m) Activity (10m) Quiz (5m) Wrap-up (5m)

A. Introduction and Review (5 minutes)

This section serves to capture students' attention and introduce the topics for the day. Use the hook to generate curiosity about the tools they use daily.

Hook

Begin by projecting a blank Google Docs screen. Ask the students: "What do you see on this screen? What do you think each button does?"

Motivation

"Today, we're going to become experts on the basics of word processing without even touching a computer! We'll learn how to format text and create organized lists. We'll also take a quick look at a new and exciting topic: what is AI?"

This section is for direct instruction. Use the interactive examples below to demonstrate key word processing features to the students in a clear and engaging way.

Introduction to AI

Switch to this topic. Ask the class: "Have you ever asked Siri or Google Assistant a question? Have you noticed how Netflix suggests movies you might like? This is a form of AI!" Then, provide simple, relatable examples like voice assistants, email spam filters, and social media recommendation systems.

C. Collaborative Activity (10 minutes)

This section outlines the hands-on group activity. Start the timer to help keep the class on schedule while students work on the paper-based task.

10:00

The Word Processing Worksheet

Divide the class into small groups and distribute a simple worksheet with the following sections:

  1. A heading (e.g., "Our Favorite Filipino Snacks") and a blank space below it.
  2. A space to create a bulleted list of "Filipino Values" with a title.
  3. A space to create a numbered list of steps for a simple task (e.g., "How to prepare a simple Filipino dish like Adobo").

Instruct groups to use different colored pens to "format" their text, simulating the use of a word processor.

D. Quick Quiz and Evaluation (5 minutes)

Use this interactive quiz to quickly assess student comprehension. Display one question at a time to maintain focus.

E. Wrap-up and Assignment (5 minutes)

Conclude the lesson by summarizing the key takeaways and presenting the homework assignment clearly on the screen.

Summary

Briefly recap the key takeaway: "Hardware, software, and the operating system are a team. Hardware provides the physical power, software provides the instructions, and the OS makes sure everyone works together smoothly." (Note: This summary seems to be from a previous lesson plan, a better summary would be: "Today we learned that word processors are powerful tools for creating and styling documents, and that AI is already a part of our daily lives in many helpful ways.")

Assignment

"Imagine you are writing an essay for another subject. On a sheet of paper, design the title of your essay and the first paragraph. Use different colors to show how you would format it (e.g., one color for bold, another for a different font). Then, create a bulleted list of 5 key points you would discuss in the essay."
